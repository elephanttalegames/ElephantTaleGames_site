Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 8, 2026

At CrownWill, operated by ElephantTale Games, we respect your privacy and provide users with the ability to request deletion of their data at any time.

🔹 General Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your data from our systems, you can request deletion by contacting us directly.

📧 Email: elephanttalegames@gmail.com

Please include the following details:

  • Your name
  • Your user ID (if available)
  • The name of the app: CrownWill

🔹 Facebook Data Deletion

If you signed in using Facebook, you can remove your data by following these steps:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings & Privacy
  2. Click on Settings
  3. Select "Apps and Websites"
  4. Find CrownWill
  5. Click "Remove"

This will remove our app’s access to your Facebook data.

🔹 Data We Delete

  • Account-related information (name, profile data)
  • Game progress and saved data
  • Facebook profile data (if applicable)
  • Any stored identifiers linked to your account

🔹 Processing Time

We process all deletion requests within 7 business days.
